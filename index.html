Stitching Hope

Connecting Senior Living Centers & Homeless Shelters Through Crochet & Kindness

Our Mission

We are a charitable organization bringing together two communities to mutually benefit: senior living centers and homeless shelters. Through crocheting and knitting events, we aim to foster connection, purpose, and generosity — donating handmade blankets, hats, and socks to those in need.

Partner Organizations

Currently, we are partnering with senior living centers that already have crochet or knitting groups as well as homeless shelters to donate clothing to. If you would like to partner with us, please reach out!
