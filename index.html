Welcome to Stitching Hope

We connect senior living centers and homeless shelters through crocheting and knitting. Together, we build community, purpose, and hope.

Our Mission

Our mission is to unite two communities — senior citizens and the homeless — through the act of creating and donating handmade items such as blankets, hats, and socks. By fostering connection and purpose, we aim to leave our world better than we found it.

Partner Organizations

We are proud to partner with senior centers and homeless shelters in our community. As we grow, we will showcase our partners here.
