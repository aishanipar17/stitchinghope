Stitching Hope

Connecting communities through crochet and kindness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give seniors a renewed sense of purpose through crafting, while providing essential clothing items to the unhoused community. Stitching Hope bridges generations and communities with every stitch.

Our Story

Stitching Hope began with a mix of frustration, compassion, and inspiration. While working as a medical assistant in geriatrics, Khushi often heard residents express their sadness and isolation. They felt a lack of community and purpose, as if their hobbies were just ways to pass the time.

At home in Philadelphia, she also saw the unhoused community struggling without even basic necessities in the cold. Everything shifted one holiday break when Khushi and her younger brother, Krish, discovered piles of his old crochet projects — hats, scarves, and trinkets he’d made since fourth grade.

What seemed like “extra stuff” to them could be life-changing for someone in need. Realizing that many seniors also loved knitting and crocheting, a vision formed: connect people who love creating with people who need warmth, care, and community.

From that idea, Stitching Hope was born — a bridge between generations, communities, and acts of kindness.
